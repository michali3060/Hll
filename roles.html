Roles
How to play

Commander

Focuses on the overall battle by coordinating squads, deploying support abilities, and keeping the team supplied with vehicles and spawn points.

Tasks

  • Controling entire team
  • Communicating with officers and giving them orders
  • Managing resources
  • Dropping air support, supply drops, and bombing runs

Equipment

Riffle or SMG, Pistol, Smoke grenades, Shovel, Watch, Binoculars, Bandages

Officer

Keeps the squad organized, chooses where the squad attacks or defends, and creates safe respawn locations for teammates.

Tasks

  • Building Outposts
  • Building Garrisons
  • Leading the squad
  • Communicating with other squads and Commander

Equipment

Riffle or SMG, Pistol(loadout ability) , Frag grenades, Smoke grenades, Shovel or Knife, Watch, Binoculars Bandages

Rifleman

Reliable all-purpose infantry role that fights on the frontline and keeps teammates supplied with ammunition.

Tasks

  • Fighting in all situations
  • Supplying ammunition
  • Helping engineers with building structures

Equipment

Bolt-action or semi-auto rifle, Frag grenades, Smoke grenades, Knife Hammer, Ammo box(loadout ability) Bandages

Assault

Specializes in aggressive close-range combat and pushing enemy-held positions.

Tasks

  • Fighting in close combat
  • Clearing buildings and trenches
  • Helping engineers with building structures

Equipment

SMG, Frag grenades, Smoke grenades, Satchel charge(loadout ability), Knife(loadout ability), Hammer Bandages

Automatic Rifleman

Uses automatic weapons to pressure enemies and support advancing teammates with sustained fire.

Tasks

  • Providing suppressive fire
  • Holding points
  • Helping engineers with building structures

Equipment

Automatic rifle or SMG, Frag grenades or Smoke grenades, Knife or Shovel Hammer Bandages

Machine Gunner

Locks down open areas with heavy suppressive fire and prevents enemy movement.

Tasks

  • Providing suppressive fire
  • Holding points
  • Destroying enemy light vehicles
  • Defending
  • Helping engineers with building structures

Equipment

Heavy machine gun, Pistol (loadout ability), Knife or Shovel, Hammer, Bandages

Medic

Keeps soldiers in the fight by reviving wounded teammates and reducing squad casualties.

Tasks

  • Fighting in all situations
  • Reviving fallen teammates
  • Setting up smoke cover

Equipment

Rifle SMG or Pistol, Smoke grenades, Knife or Shovel Bandages (many), Medical kits

Support

Provides the supplies needed for building important structures and maintaining the squads effectiveness.

Tasks

  • Fighting in all situations
  • Supplying resources and ammunition
  • Helping engineers with building structures

Equipment

Rifle or SMG, Flamethrower (loadout ability), Frag grenades, Shovel, Ammo box(loadout ability), Resources box, Hammer, Bandages

Engineer

Strengthens defenses, deploys mines, and helps the team by constructing resource-generating structures.

Tasks

  • Fighting in all situations
  • Placing and building Nodes
  • Placing and building Fortifications
  • Placing anti-personnel and anti-tank mines
  • Repairing vehicles
  • Destroying enemy armoured and light vehicles

Equipment

Rifle or SMG, Satchel charge (loadout ability), Anti-personnel mines and Anti-tank mines, Knife or Shovel, Wrench, Hammer, Torch, Bandages

Anti-Tank

Tracks and destroys enemy armor before it can threaten infantry or friendly vehicles.

Tasks

  • Fighting in all situations
  • Destroying enemy armoured and light vehicles

Equipment

Rifle or SMG, AT launcher or anti-tank rifle, Anti-tank mines, Frag grenades, Knife Bandages

Sniper

Eliminates dangerous targets from long range and creates pressure on enemy positions.

Tasks

  • Fighting in long range combat
  • Eliminating enemy artillery crews
  • Destroying enemy nodes
  • Providing reconnaissance

Equipment

Sniper rifle, Pistol (loadout ability), Anti-personnel mines (loadout ability), Frag grenades or Smoke grenades, Shovel, Hammer Bandages

Spotter

Guides the recon squad, scouts enemy activity, and disrupts enemy operations behind the frontline.

Tasks

  • Fighting in all situations
  • Spotting enemy positions
  • Helping sniper locate targets
  • Building outpost
  • Building garrisons
  • Eliminating enemy artillery crews
  • Destroying enemy nodes
  • Providing reconnaissance

Equipment

SMG or rifle, Frag grenades or Smoke grenades, Shovel or Knife Binoculars, Watch, Flare gun, Bandages

Tank crew

Operates tanks to break through enemy lines, support infantry, and control key areas of the map.

Tasks

  • Operating the tank
  • Destroying enemy armoured and light vehicles
  • Repairing vehicles

Equipment

Pistol or SMG, Torch, Frag grenades, Smoke grenades, Shovel, Wrench, Hammer, Torch, Bandages

Artillery crew

Operates long-range artillery guns to bombard enemy positions, destroy defenses, and support friendly attacks from far behind the frontline.

Tasks

  • Operating the artillery
  • Providing indirect fire support
  • Building nodes
  • Building reinforcements
  • Destroying enemy armoured and light vehicles

Equipment

Rifle or SMG, Frag grenades, Smoke grenades, Shovel Wrench, Hammer, Torch, Bandages
