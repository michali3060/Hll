How to play

Hell Let Loose is a multiplayer World War II tactical shooter focused on realism, teamwork, and large-scale battles. The game was originally developed by Black Matter after a successful Kickstarter campaign in 2017. The developers wanted to create a realistic WWII experience where communication and strategy were more important than fast arcade-style gameplay.

The game entered Early Access on Steamin 2019 and quickly became popular because of its 100-player battles and immersive atmosphere. Players fight on large maps inspired by real historical locations such as Normandy, Carentan, Stalingrad, and Kursk. The game also introduced important strategic mechanics like Garrisons and Outposts, making teamwork essential for victory.

In Hell Let Loose, players can take many different roles including infantry , tank crews , recon units, artillery, engineers, and commanders. Each role has a specific purpose on the battlefield, and success usually depends on communication and coordination between squads rather than individual skill alone.

A major moment in the game's history happened in 2022, when Team17 acquired the Hell Let Loose franchise from Black Matter and took a larger role in development. The takeover created mixed reactions in the community, but the game has continued to receive updates and remains one of the most popular tactical WWII shooters today.
